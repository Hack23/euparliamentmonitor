## 📊 Likelihood Scale: ISMS → EU Political Probability

| ISMS Likelihood | Political Probability | EU Parliamentary Analogy |
|:--------------:|:--------------------:|--------------------------|
| **5 — Almost Certain** | >70% probability | Commission tables annual legislative programme |
| **4 — Likely** | 41–70% | ECR tables resolution against Commission initiative on migration |
| **3 — Possible** | 21–40% | Renew splits on key industrial policy vote |
| **2 — Unlikely** | 5–20% | Grand coalition fails on own legislative initiative |
| **1 — Rare** | <5% | EP censures the Commission (Art. 234 TFEU) |

### EU-Specific Likelihood Calibrators

- **Grand coalition dynamics:** EPP + S&D + Renew command ~60% of seats; baseline stability is high but not guaranteed
- **Conference of Presidents influence:** Political group leaders set plenary agenda, making obstruction likelihood dependent on group coordination
- **Trilogue opacity:** Likelihood of policy deviation increases during trilogue (Council-EP-Commission negotiations) due to reduced transparency
- **Pre-election cycle:** All likelihoods increase by 0.5–1 in the 12 months before EP elections (next: June 2029)

## 💥 Impact Categories: CIA Triad → EU Political Impact

| ISMS CIA Triad | Political Triad | EU Political Measure |
|:--------------:|:---------------:|---------------------|
| **Confidentiality** | **Accountability** | Failure to disclose EU policy decisions citizens have right to know |
| **Integrity** | **Policy Fidelity** | Deviation from legislative intent through implementation or trilogue negotiation |
| **Availability** | **Democratic Continuity** | Disruption to EP legislative function (plenary, committee, vote) |

### Impact Scoring

| Score | ISMS Example | EU Political Equivalent |
|:-----:|-------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Minor operational inconvenience | Committee report delayed 2 weeks |
| 2 | Limited reputational concern | Single amendment rejected; rapporteur revises |
| 3 | Moderate financial/legal exposure | Major trilogue compromise forces concessions |
| 4 | Severe reputational or operational harm | Political group splits; Commissioner challenged |
| 5 | Existential threat | Motion of censure passes; Commission resigns |
